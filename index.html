GT7 Online Dealerships

Relating to 21-03-17 (YY-MM-DD) downtime and update changes:
The GT7 server is down for maintenance right now. (A critical issue in the v1.07 update, apparently.)
Meanwhile, the internet has been melting down not just because of that but also due to the credit reward changes in said update.
If you haven't seen, the most efficient way to grind is now less than half as efficient as it was before.
(A list of reward changes is available below.)

This moves the cars in the Legend Cars Dealership further out of reach for those of us with less time to play.
Of course, I disagree with this change: legendary cars were hard enough to obtain as is, and I was expecting/hoping that rewards would increase
(so that more difficult events would match the Fishermans Ranch event in value, expanding player choice for "grinding").
But this hasn't happened, and instead we're here.
Perhaps PD will "change their mind" as they continue their rush to build v1.08, but I doubt it.


As the server is down as of the time of this site updating:
All changes for today are based on the estimates.
If the server comes back (and I get around to updating the site), it'll be corrected to match reality.


Normal info and changelog follows:
The GT7 server updates dealerships at 00:00 UTC.
This page is manually updated and may be out of date: check the last updated string next to each section.
Note: Names are displayed in English with non-ASCII characters removed or replaced with alternatives.

15/03/22 update:
- A NEW label has been added to cars that weren't in that dealership yesterday.
- Day counters have been added: showing estimates while in regular stock, and time remaining for limited stock.
Coming soon:
- more content, as seen in each section below the dealerships.
In the far future:
- pages to view historic dealership info.
- pages to see a car's price history.
